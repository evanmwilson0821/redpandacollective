Why Thought Leadership?

Thought Leaders inspire new ways of thinking, spark discussions, and inspire action. They are domain experts and have formed a unique, clear, and strong point of view.


What is Thought Leadership?

There are 3 main types of thought leadership - your first step should be to decide what mix of them is best for you:


Industry Thought Leadership
Share innovative thoughts and insights about the news, trends, and forces shaping the market(s) you serve.

Product Thought Leadership
Share best practices, strategic roadmaps, practical how-to’s, and other information that shows your product’s potential for transformation.

Organizational Thought Leadership
Share content that reflects your organization’s vision, innovations and uniqueness. This thought leadership mirrors your organization’s culture.

Studies have found that thought leadership plays a major role in differentiating organizations in the minds of customers, partners, and sponsors. It drives real business value across industries and for both B2B and B2C organizations. In a study conducted by the ITSMA, it was found that:

75% of would-be buyers say that thought leadership helps them determine which vendor to put on their shortlist.

79% of would-be buyers say thought leadership is critical for determining which providers they want to learn more about.
